Tracking—any reporting upstream without interaction, also apps that require authentication
UpstreamNonFree—patching required to keep it free
NonFreeNet—SAAS, unless it's possible to alter the app to connect to a clone
NonFreeDep—redundant, used to apply to apps that had system library dependencies
