Auto Name:AppleWifiNlpBackend
Summary:Plugin for Nlp
Description:
A  provider for
[https://github.com/microg/android_packages_apps_UnifiedNlp Nlp]
that uses Apple Corporation's database of WiFi access points 
to make your current location available to other apps. 

Location calculation is done on-device and WiFi locations are cached to 
minimize data usage.

Nlp (not yet available)
needs to be installed already as System app.
.
