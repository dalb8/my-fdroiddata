Summary:Audio filters
Description:
Handy filters for headphone users.

Does not alter all audio output:
[[ch.blinkenlights.android.vanilla]] and
[[com.andrew.apolloMod]] are compatible.

Already found in many custom ROMs.
Relies on some system libraries that are usually only found in some custom ROMS.
They are called libcyanogen-dsp.so/libreverbwrapper.so/libvisualizer.so.
